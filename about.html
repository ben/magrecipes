What is this?

This site is the end result of a decade of thinking and collecting.

Becky has been reading about cooking for more than a decade. Her magazine-clipping stack was about two inches thick when Ben noticed it. They realized this was going to be a problem; a collection of entire magazines lets you browse by season or recipe name, but a stack of paper doesn't have a spine. The clippings weren't being used, which defeated the purpose of clipping them in the first place.

This was the germ of the idea that led to the site you see before you. Not only is this a place for clippings and "someday" recipes, but it's a place to keep all our family favorites. We hope you'll find some of your new favorites here, too.
